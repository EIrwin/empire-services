Safety First

1. Electrical:

Where is the electrical box? Can you turn it off? Is the water over electrical sockets on the floor or walls? If so DO NOT TOUCH!

2. Identify the source of the water

a. Category 1: Sanitary Water Source (i.e. broken pipe, sink overflow, etc.)

b. Category 2: Contains significant contaminiation (i.e. toilet overflow (before trap), washing machine, dish washer,etc)

c. Category 3: Grossly contaminated (i.e. seware, river water, ground surface water, etc)

3. Stop the source of the water

Where is the electrical box? Can you turn it off? Is the water over electrical sockets on the floor or walls? If so DO NOT TOUCH!
